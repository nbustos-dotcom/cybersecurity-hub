My Story

I grew up in a tech-oriented family and cybersecurity pulled me in naturally. When I started learning I found a lot of confusing, outdated, or overly technical content that assumed you already knew everything. I wanted to build the resource I wish I had.

I'm currently studying Computer Science at Michigan Tech with a cybersecurity minor, practicing hands-on through TryHackMe, class labs, and personal projects. I'm also the Chief Editor of The Byte — the ASCII Club newspaper at Michigan Tech — so communicating technical concepts clearly is something I take seriously.

Why Trust This Site?

I'm not a guru claiming to have all the answers. I'm a student actively learning cybersecurity the same way you probably are — through free resources, labs, and a lot of trial and error.

When I recommend a platform or tool it's because I've genuinely used it or thoroughly researched it — not because it pays the best commission.

Where I'm Headed

My goal is to become both a SOC Analyst and eventually a Penetration Tester. I want to understand security from both the defensive and offensive sides, and I'm documenting everything I learn along the way.
