Who I Am

I'm Nathaniel Bustos — a Computer Science freshman at Michigan Technological University with a concentration in cybersecurity. I started RootAccess because when I was learning, I couldn't find honest, beginner-friendly resources that didn't try to sell me something or bury the useful information behind a paywall.

I spend a lot of my time on TryHackMe, doing CTFs, and building home labs. Everything on this site comes from actual hands-on experience — not just theory I read somewhere.

What RootAccess Is

RootAccess is a free cybersecurity learning resource built for people who are just getting started or looking to level up. Every article is written to be honest, practical, and free — the way I wish resources were when I started.
