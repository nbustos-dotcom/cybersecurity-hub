# CompTIA Security+ Study Guide: Pass for Free in 2026

CompTIA Security+ is the most recognized entry-level cybersecurity
certification in the world. Most employers list it as a requirement for
junior security roles. The good news? You don't need to spend hundreds of
dollars on courses to pass it.

I'm a CS student at Michigan Tech with a cybersecurity minor and this is
the free study plan I'd follow if I were taking Security+ right now.

## What is CompTIA Security+?

Security+ (SY0-701) is a vendor-neutral certification that proves you
understand core cybersecurity concepts including:
- Network security
- Threats and vulnerabilities
- Identity and access management
- Cryptography
- Incident response

It's recognized by the US Department of Defense and thousands of private
employers. For most people it's the best first cert to get.

**Exam details:**
- Cost: $392 (worth it for the career boost)
- Questions: Maximum 90 questions
- Time: 90 minutes
- Passing score: 750 out of 900
- Format: Multiple choice + performance based questions

## Performance Based Questions (PBQs)

PBQs are the hardest part of Security+ for most people. They're
hands-on simulations where you have to actually configure something
or analyze a scenario.

**How to prepare:**
- Set up a free lab with VirtualBox and practice configuring firewalls,
  analyzing logs, and setting up access controls
- TryHackMe rooms give you hands-on practice that directly helps with PBQs

## Exam Day Tips

- Read every question twice before answering
- Flag uncertain questions and come back
- For PBQs — skip them first, do all multiple choice, then return
- Trust your preparation — don't second guess yourself
- Arrive 15 minutes early if taking at a testing center

## After You Pass

Once you have Security+ the next logical steps are:

1. **eJPT** — practical pentesting cert, great for hands-on roles
2. **CompTIA CySA+** — if you want a SOC/analyst career path
3. **OSCP** — if you want to go deep into penetration testing

## Is Security+ Worth It?

Yes — for entry level cybersecurity roles it's the most recognized
cert you can have. Many government and defense contractor roles
require it specifically. The $392 exam fee pays for itself quickly
once you land your first security role.

The free study resources available today make it completely achievable
without spending anything on courses.
